About HITS

Design Your Future at HITS

Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS) is a prestigious institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1985, it is a Deemed-to-be University recognized by UGC and AICTE.

With a vision "To make every man a success and no man a failure," HITS offers a wide spectrum of Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral programs. The campus is known for its world-class infrastructure, international collaborations, and a strong focus on research and innovation.
